Printmaking

Printmaking includes the four disciplines of Relief, Lithography, Intaglio and Serigraphy. Individual courses are offered in each of these areas. The print workshop is fully-equipped with etching and lithograph presses, a silk screen room, acid room and storage room. A collection of student work from previous semesters is on display continuously in the studio.
