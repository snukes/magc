Ceramics

Ceramics is a studio discipline dealing with clay and the functional and sculptural forms that can be constructed from this versatile material. Students learn clay mixing, wheel-thrown and hand-built techniques, the formulation and application of glazes and methods of firing, Kiln loading and operation is included in the advanced courses. Intermediate and advanced students are expected to develop individual, personal direction in their work.
